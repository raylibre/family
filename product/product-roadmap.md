# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Landing Page
The entry point with two clear CTAs — one for families who need help, one for donors and organizations who want to give.

### 2. Family Registration & Profiles
Families of Ukrainian soldiers create verified public profiles with their story, photo, children's ages, and specific needs.

### 3. Donor Browse & Connect
Donors browse registered families filtered by need category and initiate a direct bank transfer to the family they choose to support.

### 4. Organization Support Flow
NGOs, charities, and support organizations contact the platform to arrange custom in-kind support (food, essentials, services) for specific families.

### 5. Admin & Verification
Platform administrators review and approve family registrations, manage listings, and oversee the integrity of the public family list.
