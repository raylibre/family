# Family to Family

## Description
Family to Family is a platform connecting families of Ukrainian military soldiers with donors and support organizations. Families register publicly with their story and needs; donors browse and send money directly to family bank accounts — the platform never handles funds.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Families Lack Visibility
Soldiers' families — especially children who have lost a parent — have no easy way to reach people who want to help them.
Family to Family gives every registered family a public profile with their story, photo, and specific needs.

### Problem 2: Donors Can't Find Verified Families to Support
Individual donors and organizations want to help but don't know where to find real, vetted families in need.
The platform maintains a curated, browsable list of registered families organized by category of need.

### Problem 3: Lack of Trust in Money Handling
Donors hesitate when intermediaries handle funds, fearing money doesn't reach families.
Donations go directly to the family's bank account — the platform facilitates the connection but never touches the money.

### Problem 4: Organizations Need Flexible Support Options
Charities and organizations want to offer in-kind help (food, essentials) beyond just money, but coordination is complex.
Organizations can contact the platform directly to discuss and arrange custom support packages for specific families.

## Key Features
- Simple landing page with two entry points: "I Need Help" and "I Want to Help"
- Family registration with story, photo, children's info, and specific needs/wishlist
- Public family listing browsable by need category
- Direct bank-to-family payments (platform never handles funds)
- Organization contact flow for in-kind and custom support
- Future: subscription to follow specific families, post-donation chat
